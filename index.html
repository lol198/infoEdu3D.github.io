This website serves as an educational resource, demonstrating the power of technology and the potential of computer graphics in web browsers. Using WebGL, CSS3, and HTML5, it enables the creation of interactive and visually rich experiences directly within the web browser. Through the exploration of these educational examples, learners can gain a comprehensive understanding of web graphics: how they are created, rendered in real time, and how these technologies are transforming the way we interact with digital content on the web.
